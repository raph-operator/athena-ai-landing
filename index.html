FAQ

Common questions.

Does Athena record or store my calls?
+
No. Athena processes audio locally in your browser using the Web Speech API. Nothing is recorded or stored on any server. Your conversations remain completely private.
Which platforms does it work on?
+
Athena works on any browser-based call — Google Meet, Zoom web, Microsoft Teams, Whereby, and more. If you can open it in Chrome, Athena can coach you on it.
What is Athena's coaching framework?
+
Athena uses a proprietary 7-stage question-based selling framework built around emotional intelligence and deep discovery. Instead of pitching, you ask questions that guide the prospect to sell themselves. The framework has been validated across 500+ industries and consistently outperforms traditional sales scripts.
How does the facial cue detection work?
+
Athena analyses speech patterns, hesitation, deflection, and response timing to surface non-verbal signals. Full facial recognition via camera is on the roadmap — the current version focuses on vocal and linguistic cues that are highly predictive of prospect intent.
Can I add my own talk tracks and scripts?
+
Custom talk tracks are coming soon. You'll be able to upload your own scripts, question banks, and frameworks — making Athena completely unique to your sales process and industry.
